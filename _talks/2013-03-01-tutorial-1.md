This report will focus on research pathways in cutting-edge fields of forestry research, including ecology, remote sensing, land surface processes, and sustainable development. It will explore how to identify and refine scientific questions, construct systematic research thinking, and develop innovative research frameworks within these areas. The speaker will share methods for identifying research directions and academic planning advice for graduate students, drawing on their own research experience to help young scholars better navigate the initial stages of research and master the core thinking and methodology of scientific research.
